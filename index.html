about us

"dawood" is an amazing pastry shop that brings you a delicious and unique experience in the world of sweets. The shop offers a wide range of hand-crafted sweets with great care and using the freshest and finest ingredients. When you enter the store, you will find yourself in a world of colors and delicious scents. The dessert menu on offer ranges from cakes, biscuits and fresh pastries to fine chocolates and delicious oriental sweets. No matter if you want to buy something to eat in the shop or to enjoy at home, you will always find various options that cater to all tastes.
